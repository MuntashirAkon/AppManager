<b>App Manager</b> is a complete and easy-to-use solution for managing your applications. In addition to basic app uninstalling/enabling/disabling/etc. functionality, it provides many other useful features, such as
<ul>
    <li>Listing, launching and blocking components of the app</li>
    <li>Managing permissions for all of your applications</li>
    <li>Blocking ads, trackers, and dangerous permissions from your apps</li>
    <li>Backing up your app data</li>
</ul>
And <b>much more!</b>

<i>It basically combines the features of 5 or 6 apps that any tech-savvy person needs to use in order to have a life.</i>
